Caprese Sandwich

Fresh Italian sandwich with mozzarella, tomato, basil, and balsamic glaze.

  • 1 ciabatta bread
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons pesto
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Slice the ciabatta bread in half lengthwise.
  2. Brush both sides lightly with olive oil.
  3. Spread pesto on the bottom half of the bread.
  4. Add mozzarella slices, tomato slices, and fresh basil.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  7. Close the sandwich, slice, and serve.
Calories 420 kcal
Protein 18 g
Carbs 36 g
Fat 20 g
  • Grill the sandwich in a panini press for extra crispiness.
  • Use heirloom tomatoes for more flavor and color.
  • Add a slice of prosciutto if you prefer a non‑vegetarian version.
